Combo-Breaker

Do you think you can guess the combination?

This object of the game is to guess the combination that the computer has set.
There are three numbers in the combintation. Each number is between 0 and 10.

How do you play?

  • You will be asked for three numbers

    • (Only numbers 1-10 are allowed)

  • Enter the numbers one-by-oe
  • Ths computer will tell you if you guessed the right number.

You only get one try! Good luck!
